How to Play

Exact Score — 5 pts

You predict the exact final scoreline. E.g., match ends 2–1 and you predicted 2–1 → 5 points.

Correct Result — 3 pts

Right outcome (Win/Draw/Loss) but wrong score. E.g., you said 3–1 but it ended 1–0 → still a home win → 3 points.

Correct Total Goals — 1 pt

Total goals match your prediction but result is wrong. E.g., you said 2–1, it ended 0–3 (both 3 goals) → 1 point.

Miss — 0 pts

Wrong result and total goals don't match. Better luck next match!

Champion Bonus — +25 pts

Pick the tournament winner before it starts. If your team wins, you get a massive 25 point bonus.
