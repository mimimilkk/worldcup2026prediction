How to Play

Exact Score — 5 pts

You predict the exact final scoreline. For example, if the match ends 2–1 and you predicted 2–1, you earn 5 points.

Correct Result — 3 pts

You get the right outcome (Win / Draw / Loss) but the wrong score. E.g., you said 3–1 but it ended 1–0 — still a home win, so 3 points.

Correct Total Goals — 1 pt

The total goals in the match matches your prediction but the result is wrong. E.g., you said 2–1 (3 goals) and it ended 0–3 (3 goals). 1 consolation point.

Miss — 0 pts

Wrong result and total goals don't match. Better luck next match!

Champion Bonus — +50 pts

Before the tournament starts, each member picks their winner. If your team lifts the trophy, you get a massive 50 point bonus added to your total.

How to Use

1. Add all members under Predictions.
2. Each member enters their predicted score for every match.
3. Admin enters the actual result.
4. Points calculate automatically — check the Leaderboard!
